<p> Who was Benjamin Paddock? Las Vegas gunman's Dad and FBI wanted bank robber

<p> BENJAMIN Hoskins Paddock carried out a string of armed bank robberies in the US in 1960. His escape from a Texas jail earned him a spot on the FBI's Most Wanted list between 1969 and 1977 when his Las Vegas shooter son Stephen Paddock was a teen.
